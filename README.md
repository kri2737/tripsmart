## 📌 About

TripSmart is a full-stack travel planning web application that helps users plan their trips effortlessly. Simply enter your destination, number of days, number of people, and budget — and TripSmart will generate personalized hotel and restaurant suggestions along with a complete day-by-day itinerary.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🔐 **User Authentication** — Secure signup and login system
- 🗺️ **Smart Trip Planning** — Enter destination, days, people, and budget to get a full plan
- 🏨 **Hotel Suggestions** — Get hotel recommendations based on your budget
- 🍽️ **Restaurant Suggestions** — Discover local restaurants at your destination
- 📅 **Itinerary Generator** — Auto-generated day-by-day travel itinerary
- 💾 **Save Trips** — Save your planned trips for future reference
- 🗑️ **Delete Trips** — Manage your saved trips easily

## 📸 How It Works

1. **Sign up / Log in** to your account
2. Enter your **destination, number of days, people, and budget**
3. Get instant **hotel & restaurant suggestions** with a full **itinerary**
4. **Save** your trip for later or **delete** it anytime
